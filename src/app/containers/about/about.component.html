Everything You Need to Know About SAAT Zoom Calls

Based on the responses to the poll I posted a few days ago, here’s some information that will hopefully help those wanting to join the zoom calls for the first time! (Sorry in advance, it’s quite long!)

The basics:

The Wednesday zoom calls start at 8pm EST and the Friday ones start at 9pm EST.
Matt Delatour usually hosts the Wednesday zoom calls (with tonight as an exception because Matt Krieg will be hosting). Ming Yan Ullman, Patrick McGrail and I will be hosting the weekly Friday calls starting next week!

How they work:

Although the zoom calls have a set start time, you can drop by whenever you’re free! They literally last for several hours (I think the record is ~13 hours for the longest one). You can even join, stay for a bit, then leave and come back later too!
Once there’s a decent amount of people on the call, the host will set up breakout rooms (anywhere from around 4-12 people in each one). Sometimes the breakout rooms have a timer (ex. 60 minutes) and sometimes the host will just decide when to end them/switch up the people.

How to join:

The post with the zoom link will usually be posted in the group either the day of, or the day before its scheduled date. If anyone has trouble finding the posts with the zoom links, just look up “zoom” or the host’s name in the search bar for this group and click “most recent” to filter the search results. It’s way faster than trying to scroll through all of the posts.

Tips for first-timers:

  • Feel free to tell people that you’re new when you join
  • Introduce yourself if you’re comfortable (ex, name, where you’re adopted from, where you are now, age etc.)
  • Don’t be afraid to ask others to introduce themselves (works better in the the breakout rooms)
  • If you join and there’s a blank white screen, then breakout rooms are in sessions and just wait a couple of minutes to be assigned to one

Frequently asked questions:

  • Do I need a zoom account to join? Nope, just click the invite link and it will take you right to the call!
  • Do I have to keep my video on? No, if you feel more comfortable with just your audio on, that’s fine too!
  • What do you all talk about? A diverse range of topics, both chill and sometimes serious!

Other zoom events:

  • Sometimes people will spontaneously create zoom calls on days/times other than Wednesday and Friday evenings so keep an eye out for those.
  • Occasionally Tom Greening hosts AfterDark zoom calls (18+). You can search those up in the group to find his old posts and read about what they are.
  • Subtle Asian Adoptee Dating also hosts zoom events for those looking to make new friendships and maybe something more (most events are 18+).
