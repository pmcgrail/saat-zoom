Breakout Room Help

Introductions

If you find yourself in a breakout room with people you haven't had a chance to interact with yet, our typical introductions include name, age, pronouns, where you're adopted from, where you live now, and what you're doing for school or work. You can include as much or as little information as you feel comfortable sharing.

Ask for Help

If at any time you feel uncomfortable in a breakout room, you can return to the main room by pressing Leave Room (it will give you the option to leave the breakout room or leave the call completely). You can also press the Ask for Help button to summon the host to your breakout room and then message them privately, or reach out via Facebook Messenger.
